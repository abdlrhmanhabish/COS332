=================================================
While RFC 5321 dictates how the mail is transferred, RFC 5322 dictates how the email text itself must be formatted. 
Basic scripts only send the 'From', 'To', and 'Subject' lines. However, Section 3.6 of RFC 5322 explicitly states that 
the Origination Date field is legally mandatory for a valid message. Therefore, we used Java's ZonedDateTime to dynamically 
inject the Date header in the required RFC 1123 format. We also added a generated Message-ID, which Section 3.6.4 highly 
recommends to prevent modern spam filters from rejecting our raw packets
